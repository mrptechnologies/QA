The Municipal Insurance Company of America (MICA) was formed in Chicago, Illinois in 1946 as the successor to the Municipal Employees Insurance Association (founded 1937). During subsequent years, the Company has underwritten individual life, group, and credit life insurance; annuities; health insurance; and dental insurance.
